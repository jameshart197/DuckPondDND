Weather in Xandros

The Weather of Xandros is a being all on its own. With consistent ashfalls, acidic rain, lightning storms so powerful they spawn beasts of primal elemental nature, blizzards that freeze landscapes within minutes and droughts, there is little the inhabitants of Xandros do not have to contend with at some time or other. Although the ashfalls have lessened over the centuries since the Sundering, they still occur consistently throughout the year. Weather also pays little attention to the hemispheres or what one would expect of natural weather progression. It snows as much at the equator as it does at the poles, and droughts occur seemingly by chance and can last years. Due to this unpredictability of the weather, those who can manipulate the weather on a smaller scale are highly valued.

People of Importance in Xandros

There are many important people within the world of Xandros, but most of the following pertain to the campaigin Treasure Rats. As such, if you are seeking to use this world for your own campaign, or are playing in a different campaign relating to this world, you may find that some of the following people are not even present in your game.

The Crew of the Scarlet Rose

The Scarlet Rose is the ship on which the Treasure Rats campaign begins, and throughout the campaign become regular companions and notable allies of the party. Below, a short summary of each is given.

Ship Captain and Pilot, Cleo is friendly and fun, but determined. She is a Half-Elf Circle of the Air druid, with a familiar called Lance, the Falcon. She is savagely independent, reckless at times and very intelligent. She is known for her daring passages through the Walking Mountains and the Knife Peaks and her strong magic. She is witty, sarcastic, sharp and daring, and whilst she has a kind heart she leads on the ship without question.

The Ship’s repair bot, S3 is extremely willing to help to the point of irritation, exceptionally clumsy and very formal in his speech. He is occasionally faulty, but he’s been with the Kirkland family a long time and is very loyal. He is programmed in mechanics and repairs more recently, though Cleo thinks she was ripped off. He was originally the family’s butler Automaton, and performs both roles equally badly.

The Ship’s Chef, vice-captain and Cleo’s right-hand-woman, Brandy is sexist, has no issue with being demanding or difficult, regards everyone as her tool to use as she best sees fit. When Cleo isn’t in the room, Brandy sees her word as Law. She struggles with humour and sarcasm, being very direct and very efficient. Cleo regularly makes subtle jokes at her expense. Is very insecure about her masculine demeanour.

Cleo’s best friend, Lorelei is a High elf with a lot of money, various unusual interests and hobbies and absolutely no interest in the day to day running of the ship. Regularly found lazing around reading or debating politics and philosophy with Cleo, she is not technically a crew member, though her financial assistance keeps the Scarlet Rose afloat. Her relationship with Cleo is often more than just friendly, though there is little chance of something monogamous where Cleo is concerned.

Nabrak is an easily distracted but exceptionally intelligent Rock Gnome who failed to inherit the magical powers usually ascribed to his race. He is mostly a scientist and repairman, and his knowledge of flight paths, navigation and air currents is second only to Cleo’s. He is always inventing something new and equally unfinished.

It is easy to dismiss Orc Yot as stupid, slow or unaware, but he is a loyal member of the crew, a reliable steersman and a good friend to the Kirkland family. He has inherited some Elven blood in his past and is already well into his eighties, and looks no older than thirty. Although very strong, he has little interest in battle, preferring instead to focus on the Ship and his collections. Yot tends to be fairly quiet and private.

Hireable Sidekicks

The most consistent of Cleo’s hirelings, Cora is a youthful halfling with a love of adventure, an exceptional talent for healing and a total fear of heights and flying. Nevertheless, she regularly overcomes this to travel with the ship. She is a healer, knows how to heal mechanicals and biologicals, and has a strong belief in her Gods that guides her well in encounters with evil.

A Tortle once of the Old Guard of Laketon, Sarge is still known to everyone by his rank. Never seen without his sword, pistol and shield, Sarge is a hardened soldier who only hires out when its dangerous and there’s a good chance of treasure. Cleo finds his habits of spitting and drinking too much distasteful. Fighter, Tank, Survival knowledge.

Hank is a gunslinging Vampire who wants to see the whole world, and is trying to write a book about all the monsters in the world. Whilst Cleo thinks he’s stupid for even attempting such a thing, and finds his experiments on the dead “for science” abhorrent, he is a strong fighter to have in your corner. Gunslinger, Mercenary, Rogue abilities. Hank has been given cybernetic upgrades by Branhaus as an experiment due to his vampiric constitution.

A dashing drow rogue with a relaxed sense of moral duty, fast fingers and an eye for the finest treasure. Jaxxus is a strong combatant and a quick mind, but although he can be quick to take more than his fair share of the treasure, Cleo’s good relationship with Jaxxus is likely due to his good looks and centuries of practice in the skills of a lover.

Dex is a Kobold who has an unhealthy obsession with fire. He’s a quick and intelligent mage with a complete lack of common sense. With his trademark “BOOM” and his passion for fire, even upon her wooden airship, Cleo is not the biggest fan of taking Dex along, but understands that when it comes to large numbers of enemies, few are more efficient than the pyromantic Kobold.

Calendar

The calendar of Xandros keeps to the old system given by Oghma. The Calendar is split into twelve months, each lasting three tendays, or thirty days. There are an additional five days, falling between months, bringing the total number of days in most years to 365. The months and festivals are detailed below;

Calendar of Xandros

Ches The Claw of Sunsets

Festivals of Xandros
Midwinter Between Hammer 30 and Alturiak 1
Spring Equinox Ches 19
Greengrass Between Tarsakh 30 and Mirtul 1
Summer Solstice Kythorn 20
Midsummer Between Flamerule 30 and Eleasis 1
Shieldmeet Day after Midsummer, once every Four Years.
Autumn Equinox Eleint 21
Highharvestide Between Eleint 30 and Marpenoth 1
Feast of the Moon Between Uktar 30 and Nightal 1
Winter Solstice Nightal 20

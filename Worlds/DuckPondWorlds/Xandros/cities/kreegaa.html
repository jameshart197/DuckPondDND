Kreegaa

Built within a sliver of Elysium, adjacent to a small pocket of Celestia, Kreegaa is home to some of the greenest lands in all of Xandros. Jealously guarded by the fierce Aaba and Thri-Kreen, this is where they built their home after arriving from a far away realm during the Sundering. The architecture within these lands is a little simplistic, but no less magnificent than the greenery surrounding it, with the Insectoid races building large hives in pyramidal shapes hiding their food stores and treasuries at the very centre.

Thought to be a remnant of an old grove, twisted by the forces of Pandemonium, passing through these groves is near impossible for most races, and as such, most can only visit the lands of Kreegaa by Airship.

Kreegaa is a peaceful land designated by the two Queens, one of the Aaba and one of the Thri-Kreen. The people are rigidly traditional and incredibly loyal to their species, making the way of life in Kreegaa very simplistic, but fair and balanced. There is no crime in Kreegaa, and those lucky enough to be allowed to live here who are not of the insectoid races find it close to living in paradise.

The Economy of Xandros

lthough for the most part, the economy in Xandros resembles other games, in that Copper, Silver, Gold and Platinum pieces are used for trade all over, and prices are fairly standard all over, it should be noted that certain items are much higher in value than they once were. Whilst some of these are the obvious resources that certain cities lack, there is also a huge demand for diamond dust, which powers many of the technologies of the modern era. Also required are coals, oils and other burnable resources to power the many steam contraptions of the time. Metal is consumed in huge quantities and magic is less rare than it once was.

It should also be noted that some arts see less focus than was once applicable. You may find potions more expensive for example, and because of the lack of knowledge of the Wilderness, perfect maps, guides and travel stories of the places in between are extremely valuable. Trade with the Scavenger Townships will often be very different to the economies within cities, each having their own price system and sometimes their own currency.

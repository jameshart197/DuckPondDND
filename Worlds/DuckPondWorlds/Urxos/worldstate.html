State of the World

Urxos is a highly technologically-advanced world where there is generally a technology-based solution to every problem. The world is very much divided; different countries or nation-states are at different stages in development, though the least developed of these is still far beyond Earth of the present day. Due to the plethora of technological solutions, magic and other traditional methods of problem solving, warfare, and comfort have fallen by the wayside in favour of new tech.

Urxos is an extremely diverse world; in a single city there are dozens of different races, a tremendous wealth disparity, completely different living conditions, varying methods of transport and a wide spread of magical and technological ability. In Anarkesia, there is very little discrimination in terms of race, capability, or employment. However, there is a vast class divide, not only in terms of wealth but also between varying amounts of opportunity based on location, family history and the level of technological enhancement an individual has.

Despite the significant class disparity, even the worst-off individuals in Anarkesia live in an environment that is superbly clean with atmospheric protection systems, and have complete safety from the threats of the wilderness. Almost no humanoids live in the wilderness outside the main cities of Anarkesia, or even in the whole of Urxos. This is because the dangers of the Wilderness include untamed elementals, roaming giants, wild magic and highly advanced and mutated wild beasts and monsters. This is in addition to the deadly weather, inhospitable environments and complete lack of sustainable nourishment.

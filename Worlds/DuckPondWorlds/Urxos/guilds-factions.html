Guilds and Factions in Urxos

There are countless factions and guilds throughout the world of Urxos who hold power in their own ways. Some hold power only within the city they exist in and some spread their power throughout the entire world. These are some of the more well known factions that the party might already be aware of prior to the campaign start.

The Mystix are a union of mages throughout the land of Urxos who answer only to themselves. Their primary goal is the furthering of magical studies and the control of Fiends, Celestials and Devils upon the lands of Urxos, however they are involved in many other activities. One such activity has recently become a priority, the control of Bastoose. This drug is especially dangerous to magic users and therefore the Mystix have done their best to shut down all channels to this drug. Beyond this the Mystix are also involved in architecture and building, resource management and fertilization, enabling of transport, travel and intercontinental communication and the restriction of Electrolosis technologies that are deemed a danger to international security.

The Kandrosian Guard are a group of knights of goodly weal. These knights are usually found wearing full armour, and carry a shield, a lance, and a rifle. This guard works directly for the Emperor of Kandros, although they are also spread across Urxos, doing his bidding and protecting those in need. It is difficult to estimate the numbers of the Kandrosian Guard, but at least a thousand are always stationed within Nekson itself. The guard is made up of a wide variety of races, and boasts a large number of magic users within its ranks. They tend to be honourable to a fault, and are always fiercely loyal to the Emperor. The most renowned of the Kandrosian Guard is the Lord Protector, Lord Ser Yenlav.

The Collusion are a group formed within Anarkesia also known as “The Rebels”. This group believes in freedom above all else, and are regularly responsible for riots and revolts. Many who suffer dire poverty in Anarkesia turn to the Collusion for assistance. Operating both in the Overworld and Underworld of the cities of Anarkesia, this group has access to the best of Electrolosis technologies as well as many magic users within their ranks. Despite multiple attempts to wipe out this group, they are renowned for always rising stronger than before. The Collusion are also known for their forays into other countries to rescue those in need, who may be downtrodden by the politics of their lands.

The Hellbringers are one of the most notorious criminal syndicate in Urxos. Although technically not agents of The Horresk they are incredibly careful to work around him. They tend to be involved in the traffic of narcotics and black market magic, various standard criminal activities such as thievery and assassination, and ensuring that everyone on the streets of major cities know that they are present and active. The Hellbringers wear a symbol of flame upon their right bicep, and are almost always carrying both weapon and sword.

The Gao-Shai are the criminal syndicate of Damnation, the Underworld of Jurres. They are completely and utterly answerable to Gehenna and the Horresk, through The Lieutenant, who resides in the centre of Damnation. These criminals are organized and are usually found suited and booted wandering around Damnation in groups. They are not to be taken lightly however, and are more than happy to deliver street justice whenever necessary. Their symbol is a Dragon’s Head and they will usually have this both tattooed upon their body and upon a signet ring that they wear. Over recent years the Gao-Shai have extended their reach well beyond Jurres and into other countries, and have been seen in Perdition, Anarkesia's Criminal Underworld, though not in large numbers.

Technology in urxos

The many widespread applications of technology within Urxos are powered by a process called Electrolosis. Electrolosis is the process of converting the natural magic of the diamonds excavated from the Deep mines of Ngwala into a usable resource for advanced technologies. The majority of technology uses very little of this magic, and only requires tiny diamonds. The Anarkesians have mastered the art of grinding these diamonds into dust, allowing them to power a wide variety of simple objects, such as lights, using only trace amounts of this resource. This process was originally invented by a rock gnome named Ceroxis, and refined over more than three centuries of further developments and innovations.

In layman’s terms, the process of electrolosis involves the channelling of magic within the diamond into the whole of an item or object. This process requires somebody who understands the intrinsic workings of electrolosis and a mage of reasonable power with the capacity for intense focus. In Urxos, as a whole, electrolosis technology is fairly commonplace, used for simple items- such as flashlights, street lamps, heaters, automatic doors, and surveillance cameras. However, in Anarkesia, this technology is taken to a whole new level.

In Anarkesia, tech isn’t just used to solve large-scale issues, but to enhance comfort, for entertainment, to replace magic, and to facilitate living in ways that were previously impossible. Examples of this range from everyday citizens having the ability to fly, to individuals living in a virtual fantasy reality for almost all of their waking day. To live in Anarkesia is to be both surrounded by and powered by Electrolosis, from the implants within your head, the house you live in, to the transport you take to work.

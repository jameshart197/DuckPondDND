The Economy of Urxos

The economy of Urxos is varied from nation to nation, but every country now uses the same currency, which are called Credits. People have been splitting credits into Gold, Silver and Copper Credits to make it easier to comprehend larger sums, but it still works out to the same amount. There are three ways of carrying credit; Firstly on a Goldband, a wristband that pierces the skin slightly that directly links to your Credit Account. Secondly, on a ChipCard, which is linked directly to your account. These require face recognition to work. Thirdly, and most popular in Anarkesia, are wrist chips known as ID Chips, which are implanted into the wrist, and contain not only your personal identification and information, but a link to your account and a means of paying for any transaction.

In Anarkesia, the wealth disparity is vast. Some of the richest men and women in the world live in Anarkesia, and their wealth is comparable to that of some other countries. Huge companies such as Vega Transport, Jorgen Pharmaceuticals, Uplink, SSec, GoLive and MediCorp have budgets to rival the Anarkesian Government and are owned by a single man or woman. In comparison, many in Anarkesia, some 5% or more, are living on the streets or in dark areas of Perdition, Anarkesia's dark Underworld. Many others besides, at least 40%, are working 7 day weeks in factories or other hard labour jobs without enough money to cover their costs. Even beyond that, most of the population, another 40% or so are just about comfortable if they manage their money wisely. There is also an aristocracy, including older families of wealth, wealthy investors and lesser business owners that make up the rest of the population.

People of Importance in Sogravia

There are many important people within Sogravia, but most are a member of one faction or another and as such can be found in the relevant sections within . Those that are not included in one of those three factions are listed below for your convenience. It might be noted that if some of these are read through prior to the start of the campaign there may be minor spoilers involved, though for the most part, most people within Sogravia would know at least something of the people listed.

King Narus is an Elf who has been the King of Sogravia for just over six centuries.

The recently appointed High Priestess of the Combined Temples, Narine took over from High Priest Baden who died a year ago of old age at 226. Narine is an ambitious Aasimar who has risen quickly through the Temple Ranks due to her fervour, powerful clerical magic and determination to see the Temples act and aid in the war.

The second in command of the Combined Temples, Father Sebastian has held his role for over three centuries, and refused the post of High Priest six times. Even for a Half-Elf, Sebastian is old, and many say his life is prolonged by unnatural means. Nonetheless, he is regarded as a constant within the Combined Temples, and is known to be a gentle, loving and kind man with a reputation for justice and forgiveness.

Once the Defender General of the Dawnguard, Torgar was replaced by Anvar about 10 years ago. There are many rumours and stories as to exactly what happened and why, but the general consensus is that during a clash with Shadowveil forces in the Killing Zone between Vanhania and Imul-Daryek, Torgar made a mistake that cost hundreds of lives. As such, it is believed that he was removed from his role, though given a handsome pension for his many great services throughout the war with the Shadowveil. These days, Torgar is almost always to be found at the Last Flagon in Vanhania where he both resides and frequents the bar.

The Queen of the Elves in Zurenya.

An Elven rogue and archer who is great friends with Keilana of the Sisterhood, Starling professes no affiliation with any of the factions of Sogravia. Whilst she demonstrates both excellent physical skills and some level of magical understanding and knowledge, she is very independent and competent.

The Feurian Guard

Long ago, the Celestial Church of Feuralia was renowned for its powerful Paladin Warriors. These fearsome combatants were the reason Feuralia was able to expand its borders so far and fare so well in war. Since the coming of the Shadowveil, when Feuralia was lost swiftly to Infiltration within their highest ranks, the church of Tyr that had produced these Paladins has been rebuilding its ranks. It is only in recent times that the Feurian Guard have re-emerged into the public eye, offering a solution to the Shadowveil incursion.

The premise of the Feurian Guard is fairly simple. Much like the Celestial Church of Feuralia in the past, its goals are to bring light to the darkest of places, eradicate evil and fight against injustice. To this end the Aasimar leaders of the Feurian Guard believe that all those capable of bearing arms should be sworn into the Feurian Guard, sharing in the divine power granted by their faith and devotion to Lathander. They believe this army would be capable of taking the fight to the Shadowveil and ridding Listrus of them for all of time.

The Feurian Guard are led by powerful and charismatic paladin Skond, and his three Head Priests, Candus, Urvon and Phellin. All four of these leaders are Aasimar who have lived extended lives due to their divine powers and close bond with their God. Rumours have flown that the Feurian Guard have levied small villaged and forced them to take arms in the name of Lathander against the Shadowveil, but this has been firmly denied by Skond and his priests, and no evidence is forthcoming.

Skond is a powerful Paladin and a charismatic leader. His rhetoric is widely regarded throughout Sogravia and he has brought many followers to the Feurian Guard by sheer force of personality. The Aasimar Paladin has been alive since the Shadowveil took his homeland, and his bond with his Head Priests is one of both shared history and mutual respect. He is regarded warily by high-ranking members of all other factions for his Divine prowess and martial capabilities.

Candus is a fanatical follower of Lathander, and he has been referred to by others as the Torch for his love of fire, especially in wielding it against his enemies. He is a very capable fighter and a devout follower of Lathander who questions the beliefs of any who do not follow solely Lathander. He is arrogant, stubborn and difficult at the best of times.

Urvon is a practical man who puts the fight against the Shadowveil first, and all else second. Whilst also a devout follower of Lathander, some say he sees the religion as only a means to an end. Whether he would be content simply with ridding the lands of Shadowveil is unsure, but certainly his cruelty to those he believes infiltrated and to the Shadowveil who have been captured, makes many questions if the ends truly can justify the means.

Known as the Gentle Warrior to many, Phellin is a powerful healer and a true devout of Lathander in all aspects. Whilst he is a fervent believer in ridding the world of the Shadowveil, he seeks to uphold all tenets of the Feurian Guard equally and believes in justice and freedom. Frequently at odds with his fellow Head Priests, Skond and Phellin are thought to be very close and it is often Phellin that Skond sends in his place when he cannot be present at a meeting.

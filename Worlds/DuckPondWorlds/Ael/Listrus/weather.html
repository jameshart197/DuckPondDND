Sogravia has a fairly mild climate, with relatively warm summers and fairly cold winters. The weather in Sogravia is fairly variable, so you can experience large changes within a day. Freezing mornings give way to warm afternoons, storm clouds clear to reveal clear blue skies. It can be extremely cold in the mountains of Sogravia and to the far North, but is fairly mild in the land trapped in between. It rains more often than it doesn't in Sogravia, giving the lush, green countryside its life and making travelling fairly miserable most of the time.

The Shadowveil lands have changed greatly since the arrival of their forces, and weather has been corrupted just like every other part of the land. Huge ash storms, volcanic activity, harsh constant winds and lack of clear rain have left the land hard and grey. There can be wild storms, but little rain fall, and the dust storms can be deadly to those caught unawares. Though scouts haven't made it that deep into Shadowveil Lands for the most part, they all report the weather as unpredictable, unnatural and dangerous.

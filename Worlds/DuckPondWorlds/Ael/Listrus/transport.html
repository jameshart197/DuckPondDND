Travel throughout Sogravia is done by most on foot, by horse or wagon or along the River Durvin and its many tributaries. The country is approximately 500 miles across along the River Durvin and most roads between cities go via Centre-Point (where the rivers meet) when possible. For those with larger coin-purses there are teleportation circles available for use for the rich and important within each Magus Arcane outpost in each of the major cities.

Private teleportation or other means of magical transport are mostly limited to those wizards who are capable of doing so themselves, though there are private trips available via the Guides of the Magus Arcane at exorbitant prices. Most travellers prefer to join caravans of merchants with wagons and horses making their trips in groups for safety.

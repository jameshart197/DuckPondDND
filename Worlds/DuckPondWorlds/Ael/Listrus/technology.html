Technology was, for a long time,strictly the province of tinkering gnomes in the College of Artifice inside the Magus Arcane. Recently, however, advances have been made in the creation of magical-powered tools, items and creatures that have brought technology into a much more positive light. Whether its devices that allow for the alarming of buildings or to survey for magical intrusion, or the simple joys of magical powered lights and water-heaters, technology is becoming more present every day in the lives of Sogravia's citizens.

Technology has also advanced in other ways. In the last few decades, Warforging has become a possibility. Removing all memories from a person in exchange for rendering them invulnerable to the infiltration of the Shadowveil, many rich nobles have started to use the Warforged as expensive bodyguards and servants. Beyond this, some people have expressed interest in creating a Warforged army due to their nature and resistance to Shadowveil influence, though thus far that has only been hypothetical discussion.

The gnomes of the College of Artifice have found a far less brutal way of Warforging however. The creation of Autognomes is a long and dangerous process, and the mortality rate is fairly high. Nonetheless, many gnomes who are advancing in years risk both the pain and the chance of death to immortalize themselves in machine-built bodies to continue their fight against the Shadowveil.

Geography of Erros

The geography of Erros as a whole is varied, and contains a wide variety of biomes- from the ice sheets in the north, to the verdant forests and jungles to the south, and the archipelagos to the southwest. The land of Trevath is a land of rolling hills, open plains, lush forests, peaceful woodlands and gentle valleys.

Trevath is a medium-sized country, taking roughly a tenday to traverse from the northern border to the southern border, or eastern to western, by horse. Snaking roads extend from the capital city of Silvercrest to each of the towns across the country, with smaller villages lining the roads at various points along the routes. Most of the grasslands and plains of Trevath are rather fertile, and the many open expanses make the land particularly arable. Many people make a living working the land and selling their produce. The area is particularly notable for its plentiful vineyards, orchards and numerous farms.

The landscapes of the Untamed Lands are corrupted and broken, where dark magics, monstrous hordes and strange creatures have changed the land beyond recognition. While traversing these scarred lands, adventurers may encounter everything from bizarre swamplands, expanses of desiccated drylands, dense and ancient forests, towering sand dunes, large expanses of crags and stony ground, bubbling pools of lava and many other forms of unexpected, mysterious and broken land.

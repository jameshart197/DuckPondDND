Weather in Erros

Trevathâ€™s climate is mostly temperate and mild, with fairly consistent weather. Rainfall is rarely excessive, typically somewhere between a mild to moderate drizzle for around a third of the year, peaking during the spring. Snowfall is a rare occurrence, and even during the height of winter it is unlikely to blanket the land. The hottest days of the year during summer are typically comfortable, if a little humid. The rolling hills in particular receive large amounts of sunlight, especially during summer, making the conditions perfect for the many vineyards.

The weather of the Untamed Lands is wild and unpredictable, sometimes changing dramatically within the hour. This is a side-effect of the lingering magics that still grip the lands. Typically, conditions are both fairly hot and dry, and often plagued by strange weather phenomena. Clashing pressure fronts generate gale-force winds, kicking up dust storms across vast expanses. Dry thunderstorms may occur during the warmer days, where rain evaporates before it even reaches the ground, and huge streaks of lightning arc through the sky. Oftentimes the rains that do occur are acidic, scarring the landscape further. And during winter, hailstorms are not uncommon, sometimes with hailstones so large they can injure humanoids.

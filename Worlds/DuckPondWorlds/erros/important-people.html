People of Importance in Trevath

There are a few notable people in Trevath listed below.

The grandson of King Larion, who slayed Malakai the Lich-King, King Darius is widely revered as a goodly Paladin, a kind King and a protector of the lands. He is most adored for his "Adventurer's Bounty", a system whereby he pays adventurers, mercenaries and other heroes to slay the monstrous inhabitants of the Untamed Lands. Whilst it has been the cause of an untimely death for many would-be heroes, it has also done much to increase the safety of the Southern Border of Trevath, and brought a stream of commerce and opportunity as well.

Archmage Kardoth is an aged wizard who was alive during King Larion's time. He is widely respected throughout the realm, and although he holds no official title, King Darius is known to rely heavily on his advice and guidance. He is a powerful wizard with a kind heart and a terrifying temper when truly outraged.

Golwain is a bear of a man with a love for tales, ale and the wilderness. A Ranger of some renown, he has been known in recent times for his extensive adventures in the Untamed Lands. Claiming no bounties, instead Golwain seeks to provide areas of safety and shelter within the Untamed Lands for would-be adventurers, as well as regularly offering guidance and advice on venturing into those lands.

Neloth is the head of the Temple of the Constellation, despite his young age. He rose quickly through the ranks and is often said to be God-Touched and revered because of this. He is a gentle man who was raised as a peasant and still behaves as such in many ways, often mixing with the common folk despite his rather exalted rank.

Arynn is the current Duchess of Redkeep, her father having died fairly young and leaving her the only child. She is an adventurous sort who seems unwilling to settle down anytime soon. She has, despite many protests from her counsellors, ventured multiple times into the Untamed Lands to "see what adventurers in my duchy are risking every day of their lives". She is well liked and amiable, though perhaps a little naive in the eyes of her counsellors and other notable members of the nobility.

Ivor is a retired adventurer who is now the head of the Guild of Adventurers, based in Redkeep. He is a no-nonsense man who has little love for paperwork or administration, instead preferring to delegate such tasks. He does, however, insist on meeting all registered groups that intend to venture across the South Wall to judge them for himself. It is by his word alone that one can pass the South Wall into the Untamed Lands through Malag's Crossing.

Jurgen is the town blacksmith in Redkeep, but is also a retired adventurer, a counsellor to the Duchess and one of the most notable citizens within Redkeep. He is outspoken and loud when necessary and unwavering in his judgment of others, though he is known to be sympathetic and generous at times, especially in the presence of his wife.

Flip is the owner of the Trip Away Inn, the most popular and important inn in Redkeep, and the place where all bounty boards, quest boards and adventurer leagues in Trevath are posted and paid. Flip was once a part of the circus, and still does a little tumbling to this day. He is a light hearted, kind and fun-loving gnome with the spirit of adventure still resting inside him. He does little serving himself, instead preferring to coordinate his staff and mingle amongst the patrons bringing a smile wherever he goes.

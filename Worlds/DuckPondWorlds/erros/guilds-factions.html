Guilds and Factions in Erros

There are countless factions and guilds throughout the world of Erros, though most of them are involved in very specific endeavours unlikely to affect the average adventurer. Those that are noteworthy to adventurers and mercenaries within Trevath are noted below;

The Guild of Adventurers are more of a charter than anything else. With little influence, almost no direct purpose and very little cooperation within the guild, the purpose of the Guild is mostly to acknowledge who may pass into the Untamed Lands through Malag's Crossing.

Beyond this, there are some shared resources and connections within the guild, such as wholesale purchase and subsequent resale of various equipment and goods that can be useful to adventurers, that can benefit most parties.

The Trevation Shield are soldiers of goodly weal, mostly paladins, who answer directly to King Darius. Whilst the wider Trevathion army is made up of many soldiers with different skills and ability, the Shield are almost entirely Paladins who are extremely capable in single combat. The members of the Shield are often sent to their tasks individually or in small groups and are rarely called upon in greater numbers, and their tasks are varied and vast.

It is not unusual to see a member of the Trevathion Shield escorting a noble across the land, bringing a criminal in for questioning or battling unusual monsters on the border. They are also known to be kindly folk who will often help with smaller matters in a village, and have some level of governmental command, at least enough to make small judgments in matters of law in the minor villages and hamlets of Trevath.

The Council of Eight are eight men of power who advise King Darius directly, and are some of the most important members of the nobility within Trevath. Despite this, there are many who believe that whilst their conversations may aid the King in his thoughts, their actual influence is fairly minor, and the King is wont to make his own choices more often than not. The Council do have lands granted to them by Royal Decree but most of these are administrated by lesser nobility within smaller sections of their lands.

The Temple of the Constellation have respect from all within Trevath and are unquestioned in matters divine by most that follow the religion, which most people within Trevath do. They are the temple called upon when there are questions pertaining to the divine within the council or King's court, and as such command a lot of power and respect.

The Wizard's Consortium are far more concerned with the pursuit of knowledge and understanding of the Weave than they are with politics or any other more material matters. Many who join the Wizard's Consortium do so to aid their own learning and understanding and to share in the resources collected. There is a small section of the guild who believe that all magical artifacts found in the Untamed Lands should be studied by the Consortium before their use is permitted (or they are sold), but most acknowledge that this is fairly unrealistic. Whilst not strictly a political faction, the Wizard's Consortium is called upon by the King and his Court to answer questions pertaining to matters of the Arcane and do wield a substantial amount of influence when magic is in question.

The Hand of the Righteous are a small guild of Paladins devoted to the extermination of Evil within the Untamed Lands. They are notorious for their unwavering beliefs, notable courage and honor and lack of cooperation with adventuring parties. Whilst the guild do post requests for aid and assistance from time to time, they do not collect the bounties from the Untamed Lands and turn most of their profit over to the King or to the bettering of their knights.

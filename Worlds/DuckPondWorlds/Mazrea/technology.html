Technology in Mazrea

Technology was for a long time strictly the province of slightly mad scientists and artificers, and often regarded as more dangerous and unreliable than it is worth. Even simple clockwork devices were regarded with suspicion and doubt. Recently, however, advances have been made in the creation of magically powered tools and devices, combining very simple technology (such as clockwork or chemically-induced reactions) with magic have brought technology into a more positive light.

Many of the wealthy in cities now have their buildings alarmed by simple magical devices with the Alarm spell embedded into them, or enjoy magically powered lights and water-heaters that combine simple magics and basic technology. Technology is becoming more present every day in the lives of Mazrea’s citizens, and many (mostly the wealthy) are enjoying an increase in their luxury accordingly.

Due to the presence of the Manufactory, there is also a rise in “widely-available magical goods” which are mass-produced using artifice and magic combined. These can be adventuring gear, spellforged weapons with slots for gems that enhance the weapons or devices to simplify the casting and use of magic. Many wands, staves and other devices made in this manner have a limited number of charges that expire when used up and do not recharge, as such you may find wands and such devices more common in Mazrea, though they will not recharge.

Alchemy has come a long way within Mazrea and even the most simple of alchemists will be able to produce powerful concoctions when the ingredients are readily available. With the naturally magic land, much of Mazrea is ripe for the plants and flora that are required for such potions, though some ingredients, especially those harvested from magical creatures, are much more difficult to source.

State of the World

Mazrea is a very diverse world, with culture, geography and politics changing drastically from country to country, and even within countries. There are, however, some similarities across the whole of Mazrea. Magic is prevalent throughout the world, with cantrips being commonplace for many citizens in the world. Whilst higher level mages are not particularly common, there are many priests, sages and wizards throughout the world who have access to a fairly strong level of magic.

Similarly, the level of technology throughout the world tends to be at a similar stage of development, with very little scientific progression in the world. Whilst there are some small factions doing low level experimentation with gunpowder and some very simplistic contraptions have been created to improve functionality in farming, production and war.

There are, however, a large number of plants with magical properties throughout Mazrea, which has resulted in a very high level of alchemy within the world, especially where remedies and potions are concerned. Potions are widely available within the world, and even local alchemists are often capable of powerful concoctions if they can source the ingredients.

There are also many powerful and magical creatures in the world of Mazrea, from giants and dragons to natural magical creatures and elementals. It is thought that the inherent magic within the world contributes to the large numbers of these creatures, though the prevalence of magic use in Mazrea, including the summoning of creatures and opening of portals to other planes, may have been instrumental also.

There have been many monumental events in the history of Mazrea, from the Emergence of the Giants, to the lowering of the Veil of Chimvaya, from the Darkness of Tharizdun to the Sealing of the Moon Elves in Beldania, and the latter Wild Elf War in the same country. Vast, world-changing events seem to happen once or twice a century within Mazrea, potentially due to the powerful magic in the lands.

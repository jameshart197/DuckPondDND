Transport in Mazrea

Transport throughout Mazrea is done mostly on foot, by horse or wagon or along rivers and canals via boat. There are many boats available for river transport and booking a board upon a ship to cross an ocean is usually fairly simple. Booking board on a boat or ship will often require you to assist with simple duties, though paying more can usually avoid this. Private cabins on such boats and ships are extremely rare, and as such you will usually be expected to sleep with the other deckhands and crew. Most travellers prefer to join caravans of merchants with wagons and horses making their trips in groups for safety.

Teleportation or other means of magical transport are mostly limited to those wizards who are capable of doing so themselves, though some mages capable of such magics will offer their services at fairly exorbitant prices.

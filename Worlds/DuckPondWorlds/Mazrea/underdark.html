The Underdark

The Underdark is a vast interconnected system of subterranean caves, caverns and tunnels beneath the surface of Mazrea. Anywhere in the world you can find land, thereâ€™s almost always some form of the Underdark accessible, even on the islands of the Archipelago. Many parts of the Underdark do not directly link with others, so coming to the surface and re-entering elsewhere may be necessary in some places. Many races and creatures live within the Underdark, and it is widely regarded as more dangerous than the surface of Mazrea.

Those beneath the surfaces of Mazrea have various names for the regions of the Underdark, and there is no map of most of it, but a few names surface in all tongues;

The Abyssal Depths

The Abyssal Depths are universally known as the deepest parts of the Underdark where a bottom can often not be found, these endless plunges into darkness are often warm and inhabited by the vilest and largest of creatures. There is often magical darkness as well as natural present in these places, and nowhere else in Mazrea can more strange aberrations of nature and creatures of shadow be found. There are few humanoids found here, and those that can be found are usually best avoided.

The Shadowlands

Often a misnomer, the Shadowlands are named as such because of the presence of light, not the absence. Only in the presence of light can shadows be cast, and this large expanse of the Underdark is mostly beneath Kelaan. Wide expanses of caves and caverns lit by the strange glowing crystals indigenous to the region result in vast amounts of mining and excavation.

Deeproot

Deeproot is mostly found beneath Gallania, and is just below the deepest roots of the greatest trees. These vast caverns often house underground cities of the Deep Gnomes, Duergar, Drow and other Underdark-Dwelling races. Whilst they are the most civilised parts of the Underdark, they are often also the most dangerous, as these humanoids will often kill other races on sight, or take them as slaves.

The White Wastes

The White Wastes are a strange area of the Underdark where steam and mists are perpetually present. Found beneath the oceans and the Archipelago for the most part, these subterranean caverns often contain large amounts of water and volcanic activity, which combine to layer these caverns with thick fogs and eerie mists. These areas are avoided by all but the most dangerous or foolhardy of creatures, and few that visit return from their expeditions.

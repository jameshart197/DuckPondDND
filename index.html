Who and What is The Duck Pond DND?

I am James, a 31 year old content creator from the United Kingdom, and the Head Duck at the Duck Pond. The Duck Pond has existed as a community since February 2021 when I first started streaming on Twitch, but soon progressed into a wonderful Dungeons and Dragons community that thrives today.

Here at the Duck Pond Website, we hope to provide a central point of access for all sorts of information relating to Dungeons and Dragons. Whether it is how to best optimize your very own Dungeons and Dragons character, different methods of rolling stats, specific information relating to a fantasy world setting, be it an Official world or a world within a Duck Pond Homebrew Campaign or much else besides.

If you wish to join our community, the best way to do so is to join up with our public Duck Pond Discord. Alternatively, you can find us on Twitch or Youtube. We try and welcome everyone to our community, regadless of their background or experience with Dungeons and Dragons, but we do operate a strictly 18+ rule for all of our community. We hope that you find the site useful and enjoy your stay.
